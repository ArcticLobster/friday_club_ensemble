《星期五俱乐部》：

缘于对歌唱的共同爱好，2005年一群音乐发烧友们组成了《星期五俱乐部》。他们相聚于每月一 个星期五的晚上，唱在一起，笑在一起。他们既是歌者，又是舞者，还是乐器手和编导能手。多 才多艺是他们的特色，标新立异是他们的追求。《星期五俱乐部》于2007年和2012 年举办了两场 “音乐像册-流行节拍”音乐会，精彩的表演和与众不同的风格给新州的音乐舞台增添了一抹令人 瞩目的光亮。

《Friday Club》 :

About eight years ago, the Friday Club began with a group of people that had a shared love of music. Our members’ professional lives were busy, but each of them found ourselves longing for a place to enjoy creating music and entertainment. As it progressed, the Friday Club began incorporating different instruments and creating dances to accompany the songs. The Group participated in various shows and concerts over the years, and in 2007 and 2012, put on their own independent shows. In the Chinese community, the Friday Club is known for their unique style.
